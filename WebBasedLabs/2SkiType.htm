Skier Type

*Warning skier recommendations are based on personel experiences, general knowledge, and trusted individuals. If something is incorrect and personal damages occur, look at my mothers FB in March 2020. (I'm sorry it is the way the cookies crumbles)*

Beginner Skier

Recommended adults and children to have skies between belly button and shoulder height for less speeds, faster turning, and low demand for specific slope types. Recommend to stay on Greens or easier Blues

Intermediate Skier

Skis are typically longer and more customization with bindings available. Skiers here can traverse through Blues and Greens comfortably, easier Blacks are also recemmended to try.

Advanced Skier

Advanced Skier recommendations are based on preference. Now that you can do the whole mountain Skis should be used to suit your style best. With different variations of slopes any ski would make the difference!
